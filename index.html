What We Do

We are here to help you achieve your goals

No matter what you are looking for in a gym, we have got a membership option made for you. All GYM Land members enjoy unlimited access to their home club and the support of our friendly, knowledgeable staff anytime you need it. GL Platinum Card members receive additional benefits, including the ability to bring a guest for free and access to any of our 30 GL locations. Looking for a little extra guidance or inspiration to help you meet your fitness goals? As a GL member, you can take advantage of our free fitness training or find your motivation by reading real member experiences in our instagram group, to which you can join: @GYM.Land. It is our goal to provide a clean, safe, welcoming environment for anyone who walks through our door, and all the equipment, amenities and support you need once you are here.

What Our Members Say

Victoria Petrova

Excellent place for swimming and spa. In addition to the pool, it also has a sauna, a steam room, a steam Aroma room, a salt sauna, a Roman bath, hygienic showers and thermal beds. Soft relaxing music is playing everywhere. 3-4 hours pass imperceptibly.

Dimitar Lefterov

The gym is on three levels and even when it's 5-6-7pm. There is enough space to practice. It's relatively clean and I rarely run out of parking. Machines, weights, everything is there. The spa area is also ok.

Ralica Kashinova

Very good chain of gyms. A variety of appliances that are relatively new. Good and convenient locations to visit. As a disadvantage, we can note the large amount of people who allow to exercise shirtless and use the spa areas. In such periods it is, to say the least, unpleasant for normal training and relaxation.
